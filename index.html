PBS Guide
Workflows, tips & daily checklist
🔥 Tap each item when done. Resets automatically at midnight every day.
☀️ Morning Routine
Open Dashboard
Check break-even target, stock alerts, and supplier deadlines. Know your number before you open the shop.
Review Stock Pulse
Filter by Critical and Out-of-Stock. Reorder anything urgent via Reorder Manager in Coach tab.
Check Supplier Deadlines
Any payments due today or overdue? Open Suppliers tab — collapsed cards show balance at a glance. Pay from Supplier Debt bucket first.
Check Expiry Monitor
Stock tab → scroll past Stock Pulse. Items within 30 days are highlighted. Plan returns or promotions before they expire.
🌙 Evening Routine
Review POS Sales
POS tab → Sales History. Check Today filter. Confirm total matches the physical cash + transfer in your drawer.
Log Day in Cash Desk
Cash tab → Log a Day. Enter sales, cash + transfers received (all money in), all expenses. Run 5-Bucket allocation.
Receive Any New Stock
Snap tab → photograph pack → Save & Receive Stock. Or Stock Pulse → Receive button on any product row.
Review 5-Bucket Allocation
Set aside the Supplier Debt portion before any personal spending. This is what separates surviving pharmacies from thriving ones.
📅 Weekly (Fridays)
Export PBS Backup
Guide → Settings → Export Backup. Save the JSON file to Google Drive or WhatsApp it to yourself. One minute. Weekly habit.
Review Week Profit
Cash tab → This Week filter. Is net profit above break-even? Check the summary cards at the top. If not — investigate before Monday.
Open Dashboard every morning and evening. It is your operational command centre.
📊
Today's Snapshot (4 Cards)
Sales, Expenses, This Week, Week Profit. These update automatically when you log a day in Cash Desk. Green = profitable. Red = below target.
🎯
Break-Even Hero
Your daily sales target based on fixed costs. Green when you pass it. Set your actual costs in Guide → Settings → Break-Even Settings. The target auto-recalculates.
📦
Stock Pulse Alerts
Red = Out of Stock (losing money now). Orange = Critical. Yellow = Low. Green = OK. Tap any alert card to go directly to that product.
🏭
Supplier Obligations
Total outstanding across all suppliers with overdue count. Tap the card to open Suppliers tab and action payments.
🧠
AI Morning/Evening Brief
PBS auto-schedules briefings at 5:30 AM and 9:00 PM. Coach tab → ⚡ Generate for AI 5-priority actions. Requires OpenRouter API key (free).
Every product marked Monitor appears in Stock Pulse. No limit on products.
🔴🟠🟡🟢
Status Colours
Green = OK (above minimum). Yellow = Low (at/below min). Orange = Critical (below 50% of min). Red = Out of Stock. Set minimum stock level when adding a product in Snap.
📥
Two Ways to Receive Stock
(1) Snap tab → AI reads pack photo → tap Save & Receive Stock. (2) Stock Pulse → tap Receive on any product row. Both open the Receive Stock modal with the product pre-filled.
📋
Stocktake Mode
Tap the Stocktake button top-right of Stock Pulse. Enter your actual physical counts. PBS calculates variance and adjusts records automatically. Do this monthly.
⏱️
Expiry Monitor
Scroll past Stock Pulse. Colour-coded by time remaining: Red = under 30 days, Orange = 30–60 days, Yellow = 60–90 days. Take action before items expire worthless.
📦
Reorder Manager
Coach tab → scroll down past Priority Actions. Lists all Out-of-Stock and Low products with estimated reorder cost. Tick items, edit quantities, tap Send to Supplier to order via WhatsApp directly.
Snap is your product intelligence engine. The more packs you scan, the richer your database becomes.
🤖
AI Snap (Camera)
Tap AI Snap → photograph any drug pack → Claude AI reads the label and extracts name, strength, category, dosage form, price. Review and confirm. Then choose: Save to Database, Save & Receive Stock, or Sell Now.
🛒
Sell with Snap
After AI reads a pack, tap the purple Sell Now button. PBS sends the product directly to POS basket with name and price pre-filled. Just enter quantity and confirm the sale.
📥
Save & Receive Stock
After AI Snap, tap the green Save & Receive Stock button. Saves product to database then immediately opens the Receive Stock modal with the product name pre-filled — enter supplier, cost, quantity, expiry and save.
🔍
Search Database
Type 2+ characters to search your full product database. Tap any result to view details, edit the product, or open Receive Stock for it directly.
📷
Barcode Scanner
Tap Scan Barcode mode → hold barcode in the box. Auto-detects EAN-13, EAN-8, Code 128, QR codes. Camera uses continuous autofocus — give it 1–2 seconds to lock on.
3-step guided sales flow. Each step auto-advances to the next. The fastest POS for community pharmacy.
1️⃣
Step 1 — Search Product
Type the drug name. Matching products appear as buttons — tap to select. If the product doesn't exist, add it via Snap tab first.
2️⃣
Step 2 — Pack & Quantity
Choose pack size (Tablet / Strip / Bottle etc). Tap the Qty field — number keyboard opens immediately. Type quantity and price auto-fills. Tap Add to Basket.
3️⃣
Step 3 — Payment
Enter customer name (optional — auto-completes from Customers). Enter customer WhatsApp number to send receipt. Apply discount (% or flat ₦). Enter Cash and/or Transfer. Change calculates automatically. Tap Confirm Sale.
🧾
Receipt & Thermal Print
Receipt appears after every sale. Toggle Print ON/OFF at the top of the receipt. Print dialog opens — select your 58mm thermal printer. WhatsApp sends the receipt text to the customer number you entered.
📋
Sales History
Below the basket. Filter: Today / This Week / Month / All Time. Tap Filter for product name search or custom date range. Each sale row is collapsed — tap to expand full items, totals, and reprint receipt.
⏸️
Hold Basket
Tap Hold to park the current basket and start a new sale. Tap Held to resume any parked basket. No limit on held baskets.
Log every day in the evening. Consistency transforms data into financial intelligence.
📝
Log a Day
Enter: Total Sales (from POS Sales History → Today), Cash + Transfer Received (all money that came in today — cash and bank transfers go to the same account so record both), and expenses by category. Tap Save Day Record.
💰
5-Bucket Cash Allocation
Splits today's cash automatically: Inventory 35%, Supplier Debt 25%, Expenses 20%, Owner Draw 15%, Reserve 5%. The discipline of not mixing these buckets is what makes pharmacies financially healthy long-term.
📊
History Summary Cards
Top of Cash History shows 4 coloured total cards for the selected period: Sales, Net Profit, Expenses, Cash In. Instantly see your performance without scrolling through every record.
📅
Date Range Filter
Cash History → tap Filter → set From and To dates. Analyse any specific period. Useful for month-end summaries, tax preparation, or comparing two months.
📤
Export Reports
CSV Export: full date-range data for spreadsheet or accounting software. PDF Daily: one-page formatted summary for the selected period, suitable for printing or sharing.
