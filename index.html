PBS Guide
Workflows, tips & daily checklist
🔥 Use this checklist every day. Tap each item when done. Resets daily.
☀️ Morning Routine
Open Dashboard
Check alerts — any out-of-stock or supplier deadlines?
Review Stock Pulse
Filter by Critical and Out — reorder anything urgent
Check Supplier Deadlines
Any payments due today or within 5 days?
Check Expiry Monitor
Any items expiring within 30 days? Plan for returns or promotions
🌓 Evening Routine
Transfer Notebook to QuickBooks
Log all today's transactions from your physical notebook
Log Day in Cash Desk
Enter total sales, cash received, expenses, and any notes
Update Any Stock Changes
Add new deliveries received today via Snap or Stock tab
Review 5-Bucket Allocation
Set aside supplier debt portion before spending on anything else
📅 Weekly (Fridays)
Export PBS Backup
Go to Settings tab — Export Backup — save to Google Drive
Review Week Profit
Cash Desk → This Week — is profit trending up or down?
Open Dashboard every morning and evening. It is your command centre.
📊
Today's Snapshot
4 cards: Today Sales, Today Expenses, This Week total, Week Profit. All auto-populate when you log in Cash Desk.
💊
Stock Pulse Alerts
Red = out of stock or critical. Costing you money every hour. Reorder before anything else.
💰
Cash Allocation
Shows today's 5-bucket split. Log sales in Cash Desk first for this to appear.
🏭
Supplier Obligations
All suppliers with balances, highest priority first. Total obligations shown at bottom.
Monitor as many products as you like — no limit. Every product marked Monitor appears in Stock Pulse.
✅
Status Colours
Green = OK  ·  Orange = Low  ·  Red = Critical  ·  Dark Red = Out of Stock  ·  Red border top = Expiring within 30 days
📦
Reorder Cost Estimate
Tap Reorder on any product. PBS shows you exactly how many to order AND the estimated cost based on last recorded cost price.
🕑
Expiry Monitor
Scroll down on Stock tab. Lists all batches expiring within 90 days. Red = 30 days. Orange = 60 days. Plan returns or promotions early.
The Snap engine learns. After first entry, future scans bring up all details instantly.
📷
Real Barcode Scanner
Tap Start Barcode Scanner. Point camera at barcode on drug pack. It reads automatically — no typing. If product found, all details fill. If not, tap Add as New.
🔍
Search Database
Type 2+ letters of drug name. Tap result. All product details fill — just enter batch, expiry and qty.
➕
New Product
First time only. Fill all fields. One-time entry — every future scan brings it up instantly. Always enter cost price for profit tracking.
Log each day in the evening after transferring your notebook to QuickBooks.
📝
Log a Day
Enter Total Sales and Cash Received. Add expenses with categories. Use Day Notes for holidays, generator issues, prayer days — anything unusual.
💰
5-Bucket Allocation
Inventory 35% — Supplier Debt 25% — Expenses 20% — Owner Pay 15% — Reserve 5%. Protects your working capital every day.
📅
Custom Date Range
In Cash History, set From Date and To Date then tap Apply. Useful for comparing specific weeks or seeing any period you choose.
Tap any supplier card to auto-load into the edit form.
🏭
Supplier Cards
Full payment timeline, deadline warnings, and total obligations shown at the bottom of the list.
💳
Pay Button
Opens payment modal. Enter amount and reference. Select your banking app to pay directly. Balance updates instantly.
📋
Share Statement
Generates full statement with all payment history. Share on WhatsApp as proof of payment.
